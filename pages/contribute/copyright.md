title: Copyright guidelines
---

**It is your responsibility to ensure all content respects copyright.**

You may include quotes and images from other resources if they add value, but you must follow the guidelines below.

## Quoting from other resources

When quoting from websites or publications:

- Enclose quotes in quotation marks
- Keep the exact wording
- Link to the source
- Credit the author (if known) and resource
- Provide full citations for publications

Do not copy text and modify it slightly. If not quoting directly, express the concept entirely in your own words. Avoid long quotations.

## Images

**Most images on the web are copyrighted**, including those on social media. Before using any image:

1. **Obtain permission** (ideally in writing) from the copyright owner
2. **When in doubt**, assume you don't have permission and contact the owner

### Specific image types

**Creative Commons images:**
You must attribute the creator according to the license terms. See [Creative Commons licenses](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/).

**Stock/royalty-free images:**
Check the license to confirm use on this website is permitted.

**Institutional images:**
Verify with your institution that you can use them on ELIXIR Training SPLASH.

**Your own images:**
Follow our [style guide](style_guide). Images may be modified to match site style. All site content is published under [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).
